Process & Configuration

Standardized process flow and configuration logic based on different forming technologies.

Deep Drawing LN28 / LN34

Deep Drawing – Standard Process Flow

Blank → Drawing → Trimming
  1. Engineering review: drawing analysis & material specification
  2. Press configuration: tonnage calibration & stroke optimization
  3. Drive system selection: fast cylinder or servo control
  4. Automation integration: robotic transfer & trimming station
  5. Process validation: trial forming with video documentation

* Final configuration depends on material, thickness and product geometry.

Hydroforming LN98 / LN99

Hydroforming – Production Process Overview

Preform → Water Bulging → Finish
  1. Preform engineering: blank geometry & material flow analysis
  2. Pressure system configuration: sealing integrity & pressure profiling
  3. Wall thickness control: uniform distribution optimization
  4. Secondary operations: precision trimming & edge beading
  5. Quality assurance: surface finish specification & validation

* Final configuration depends on material, thickness and product geometry.

Fine Blanking LN32-S

Fine Blanking – Precision Control Workflow

High Precision Parts Manufacturing
  1. Tooling design: die configuration & clearance optimization
  2. Press setup: triple-action synchronization & force distribution
  3. Material handling: precision feeding system & automation integration
  4. Quality control: edge finish monitoring & dimensional verification
  5. Production stability: process repeatability & cycle optimization

* Final configuration depends on material, thickness and product geometry.
